## 👋 About Me

Graduate student at the **Tissue Engineering Lab, Faculty of Life and Medical Sciences, Doshisha University**, focused on the social implementation of AI in healthcare.

I take a **field-first, product-driven approach** — visiting clinical sites, sitting with physicians and patients to surface real problems, and shipping software that solves them end-to-end.

### 🔬 Research @ Doshisha University
- Building an app where **AI interviews psychiatric patients** and delivers structured insights to physicians, supporting their clinical decision-making.
- Developing a system that **transcribes clinical conversations in real time** and auto-generates medical records in **SOAP** format.

### 💼 Internships
- **EVeM Inc.** — Developer on a project where an **AI avatar** delivers management training.
- **Novagrid Inc.** — IT consultant providing solutions that **integrate AI into Kintone**.
